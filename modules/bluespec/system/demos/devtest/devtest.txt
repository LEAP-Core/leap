Devtest

This is a small demo program that exercises a
variety of of the virtual platform devices, i.e.,
the front panel buttons, switches and LEDs and
streams I/O. It does not use any soft connections.

It operates as follows:

     Button          Action
     ------          ------
     Ok              Load switches into LEDs
     Up              Increment LEDs by 1
     Down            Decrement LEDs by 1
     Left            Return to initial state
     Right           Finish
